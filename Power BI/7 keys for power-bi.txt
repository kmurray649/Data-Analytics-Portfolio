7 Keys for Power-BI

1. Brevity is the soul of wit
Very few visuals on each slide

2. No/very little text
Picture is worth more than words. I want people paying attention to what I'm saying.

3. Titles
	A) A summary is the worst kind of title
	a summary title describing the slide is horrible. Never use these.
All Insights need to be backed by data
	B) Insight Titles are good
	An insight is something you are trying to convey from the data
	C) Actionable Insight are GREAT, but not always possible
	Examples: Increase sales by 10%, Increase retention by 20%, Lower distribution costs

4. *Context*

Full picture of the data is important. 
We need all the data to form an insight and opinion, not just pick and choose which data we want to use.

5. Problem Statement
Always need to keep the presentation and focus on our Problem Statement. 
The Problem Statement is the START of our story. 
We must have a beginning and share the SOLUTION or END of the story.

6. Presentation Pyramid
	a) Always Lead with our conclusion
	b) Main
	c) Backup slides

7. Marinate
	If its on the slide, you should have a mastery of the information.
	If you don't know something about a question, "I don't know, let me follow up in an email after this..."
